Our Story

Crafting memories through scent since 2020

The Art of Bespoke Fragrance

MiniScent-Bar was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a signature scent that tells their unique story. What started as a passion project in Nairobi has grown into Kenya's premier destination for bespoke perfumery.

Our expert perfumers guide you through an intimate fragrance journey, helping you discover notes that resonate with your personality, memories, and aspirations. Each 30ml bottle is hand-crafted and personalized with your chosen name.

Whether you're treating yourself, gifting a loved one, or creating corporate experiences, MiniScent-Bar transforms perfume from a product into a personal statement.
